# HOTEL Ready - Brand Identity & Guidelines

**Version**: 1.0.0  
**Date**: June 2026

---

## Brand Overview

**HOTEL Ready** is a professional AI-powered staff training platform designed for the hospitality industry. Our brand represents excellence, innovation, and support for hotel staff development.

---

## Brand Values

- **Excellence**: High-quality training and consistent results
- **Innovation**: AI-powered technology for modern training
- **Support**: Comprehensive tools and resources
- **Accessibility**: Easy-to-use interface for all staff levels
- **Scalability**: Growing with organizations of all sizes

---

## Visual Identity

### Logo
- **Primary Logo**: HOTEL Ready text + icon
- **Icon**: Stylized speech bubble representing conversation/training
- **Color**: Crimson red (#b91c1c) + professional gray

### Color Palette
- **Primary Red**: #b91c1c (Confidence, Energy)
- **Secondary Blue**: #1e40af (Trust, Professionalism)
- **Accent Green**: #059669 (Success, Growth)
- **Neutral Gray**: #374151 (Text, Backgrounds)
- **White**: #ffffff (Clean, Modern)

### Typography
- **Headlines**: Bold sans-serif (Inter, Helvetica)
- **Body Text**: Regular sans-serif (Inter, Helvetica)
- **Monospace**: Code snippets (Monaco, Courier New)

## Tone of Voice

- **Professional**: Business-appropriate language
- **Clear**: Simple, jargon-free explanations
- **Encouraging**: Supportive messaging for staff
- **Helpful**: Solution-oriented approach
